Weekend Game Plan

The goal is not to overdo Disney. The goal is a fun, easy, summer-kickoff weekend: water park Saturday, cozy Wilderness Lodge evening, pool day Sunday, and home before everyone completely crashes.

No theme park tickets No huge drop slides Mobile order lunch at 12 Eat lunch at 12:30 Leave Blizzard around 4 S’mores + Water Pageant
🌊

Saturday Mood

Excitement first: chairs, best family rides, Ski Patrol, lunch, wave pool, lazy river, dessert, favorite repeats, then head to Wilderness Lodge.

🌲

Sunday Mood

Slow breakfast, resort exploring, checkout, Bell Services, pool, snacks, lunch, more pool, change, drive home.

Best mindset: If the kids are happy somewhere, stay there. This itinerary is a guide, not a strict schedule.

Day 1: Blizzard Beach Play-by-Play

Park opens at 10:00 AM. Arriving 9:00–9:30 gives you time to park, sunscreen, enter calmly, and claim a good base camp.

6:00 AM
Leave home
Kids wear swimsuits under clothes. Breakfast in the car. Keep water park bag easy to reach.
9:00–9:30
Arrive at Blizzard Beach
Park, bathroom stop, sunscreen before entering, quick group photo while everyone is fresh.
9:30–10:00
Claim chairs / base camp
Aim for Melt-Away Bay area, reasonably close to Ski Patrol Training Camp. Look for shade or umbrellas.
10:00
Teamboat Springs
First ride. Best group/family ride and lines grow later.
10:25
Toboggan Racers
Fun racing slides. Not scary, great for kids and adults.
10:45
Runoff Rapids
Tube slide with manageable thrills. If the wait is too long, skip and move to Ski Patrol.
11:10
Ski Patrol Training Camp
Zipline drop, iceberg crossing, obstacle-style play, and smaller thrills. This may become a kid favorite.
12:00 PM
Mobile order lunch
One adult orders from Lottawatta Lodge for a 12:30 pickup. Everyone can pick what they want.
12:30
Lunch at Lottawatta Lodge
Eat, hydrate, and reapply sunscreen after lunch.
1:15
Melt-Away Bay Wave Pool
Perfect after lunch. Fun, central, and easy to regroup.
1:45
Cross Country Creek Lazy River
Float, hydrate, relax, and cool down.
2:15
Dessert at I.C. Expeditions
Good time for a frozen treat or shared sundae break.
2:45
Favorite repeats
Let the kids choose: Ski Patrol, wave pool, Toboggan Racers, lazy river, or Teamboat if line is good.
3:30
Final water park stretch
Keep it flexible. Do not force one more thing if kids are fading.
3:50
Bathrooms, lockers, dry-off
Gather towels, wet bags, shoes, sunscreen, and water bottles.
4:00
Leave Blizzard Beach
Head to Wilderness Lodge for check-in, showers, dinner, and resort activities.

Day 1 Evening: Wilderness Lodge

After Blizzard Beach, keep the evening cozy and flexible. The magic is the resort atmosphere, not rushing.

4:30 PM
Arrive + check in
If rooms are ready, shower and reset. If not, Bell Services can hold bags and everyone can explore.
4:30–5:30
Check recreation schedule
Look for tie-dye, painting/crafts, pool games, campfire, s’mores, Movie Under the Stars, and Water Pageant timing.
5:30–6:30
Dinner: casual and easy
Options: Geyser Point, Roaring Fork quick service, or whatever fits everyone’s energy level.
After dinner
Campfire / s’mores
Times vary. Follow the Wilderness Lodge recreation schedule posted that day.
Evening
Electrical Water Pageant
Watch from the lakefront if timing works. Easy, charming, and very Disney.
Night
Movie Under the Stars
Pajamas, blankets, popcorn, glow bracelets. Stay as long as the kids are happy.

Day 2: Pool + Resort Day

Sunday should feel like vacation. No rushing, no extra transportation, no big agenda.

8:00 AM
Easy breakfast + coffee
Use Roaring Fork breakfast for coffee, pastries, breakfast sandwiches, Mickey waffles, or grab-and-go options.
9:00
Explore Wilderness Lodge
Lobby, geyser, lake walk, gift shop, photos. This is when the resort feels peaceful and beautiful.
10:30
Pack pool bag / checkout prep
Dry clothes, toiletries, shoes, and phone chargers should be separate for an easy end of day.
11:00
Checkout + Bell Services
Leave luggage with Bell Services if needed. Everyone can still use the pool after checkout.
11:15–12:30
Pool time
Waterslide, swim, relax. This is the heart of Sunday.
12:30
Pool lunch
Use Roaring Fork, Geyser Point, snacks, or whatever works best.
1:00–4:30
Final pool stretch
Swim, snacks, drinks, slide repeats, and relaxed adult time. Keep this unstructured.
4:30
Change + final photo
Dry clothes, one last group picture, refill waters, quick bathroom stop.
5:00
Head home
Leave before everyone fully crashes. Kids should be tired in the best way.

Blizzard Beach Map + Our Route

Use the official map if needed, but the main strategy is simple: base camp near Melt-Away Bay, do the best family rides first, then settle into central play areas.

Official Map

Our Route

Base Camp: Chairs near Melt-Away Bay / reasonably close to Ski Patrol.
Teamboat Springs: First ride at opening.
Toboggan Racers: Fun racing slides.
Runoff Rapids: Tube slide if wait is manageable.
Ski Patrol Training Camp: Zipline, iceberg crossing, kid adventure zone.
Mobile Order: Order Lottawatta Lodge at 12:00 for 12:30 lunch.
Melt-Away Bay: Wave pool after lunch.
Cross Country Creek: Lazy river reset.
I.C. Expeditions: Dessert / frozen treat break.
Final Favorites: Let the kids choose repeats until 4:00 PM departure.
